b) Location Information

c) Usage Data

We use collected data to:

  • Show relevant nearby activities

Meeto is not designed specifically for children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Parents or guardians may contact us if they believe their child has provided personal data.

We do not sell personal data.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. User Choices

You may:
